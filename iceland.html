ICELAND
            Iceland, a Nordic island nation, is defined by its dramatic landscape with volcanoes, geysers, 
            hot springs and lava fields. Massive glaciers are protected in Vatnajökull and Snæfellsjökull national parks.
            Most of the population lives in the capital, Reykjavik, which runs on geothermal power and is home to the 
            National and Saga museums, tracing Iceland’s Viking history.
            Capital: Reykjavík
            Currency: Icelandic króna
            Population: 3.38 lakhs (2017) Eurostat
            Ethnic groups (2018): 89% Icelandic; 5% Polish; 1% Lithuanian; 5% other
